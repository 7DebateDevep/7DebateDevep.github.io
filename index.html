Methodology

Welcome to the Chengdu No.7 High School International Department Debate Community website. We are one the most successful high school public forum debate communities in the nation.

Reading and Lectures

Weekly reading material will be assigned during weekends. Auto-grading exams are required to finish before attending lectures. Reading material will provide introduction to weekly topics so that students can have better understanding on the background information.

Discussion

Discussion sections will take place during week days. Discussion sections will have a moderator coming from debate team and four community members giving feedbacks, discussing weekly topics, and conducting mock rebuttal and summary.

Mock debate

Mock debate and campus tournaments will be held after community members finish writing constructive speeches and blocks. Substantially amount of mock debates will prepare community members for campus tournament and official national championship.
